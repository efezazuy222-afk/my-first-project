入境旅游

Inbound China Tours

Wuxi Lanqing International Travel Agency is a professional China tour operator and travel agency, dedicated to providing high-quality inbound tourism services for foreign tourists. We offer a wide range of China tour packages, including Yangtze Delta tours, classic China tours, and customized travel services.

Our experienced multilingual guides will accompany you throughout your journey, providing professional interpretation and cultural insights. We strive to create unforgettable travel experiences that showcase the best of China's natural beauty, historical sites, and cultural heritage.

Whether you're interested in the ancient capital of Beijing, the modern metropolis of Shanghai, the classical gardens of Suzhou, or the scenic beauty of the Yangtze River, we can tailor a tour package to meet your specific needs and interests.
